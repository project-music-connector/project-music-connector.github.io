About

Innovate, Learn, Engage.

Project Music Connector was founded in fall 2015 with the goal of democratizing music technology and presenting to children different ways that engineering can be used to create tools for artistic expression. This evolving team is composed of students primarily from Georgia Tech, united by an interest to explore, design, and build new digital musical instruments. The group has presented their creations at numerous educational workshops and demos for K-12 audiences, including at the Atlanta Science Festival, Atlanta Symphony Orchestra, Georgia Tech InVenture Day, and various schools in the Atlanta area. In addition, Project Music Connector was received Maker of Merit awards in 2015 and 2016.

Current members of the team include Alice Barbe, Kevin Choi, Beatriz Fusaro, Asimm Hirani, Abhirav Joshi, Elisha Mang, Mihir Parshionikar, Malvika Upadhyaya, and Samuel Yeomans.
