Our Team

Meet some of our makers!

Alice Barbe

Alice is a homeschooled high school student with years of classical music training, a love of building things, and a dedication for community service. She's always on the lookout for new opportunities to explore, cool projects to work on, and interesting people to talk to!

Beatriz Fusaro Guimaraes

I am a student at Georgia Tech pursuing a BS in Mechanical Engineering with a focus on automation and robotics and a minor in Computer Science. Creating new products is my true passion, and I don't spend a day without a project to work on. The machine shop is my second home, but I also love coding and design. If there is a new concept that needs development, you can count on me!

Asimm Hirani

Mechanical Engineering student at Georgia Tech who enjoys the rush of building and making anything under the sun. Asimm enjoys spending his time outside of class engineering electric cars for the Formula SAE team and rockets for the Yellow Jacket Space Program. Asimm functions on Music Connector as a mechanical designer and prototype builder.

Kevin Choi

Kevin is a 3rd-year undergraduate student majoring in Mechanical Engineering. As a student with a deep interest in robotics and autonomous systems, Kevin has worked with the Battlebots team as well as the research team for agricultural robotics. As a result of working with these teams, he has gathered experience in structural design and multidisciplinary collaboration- for example, creating an elegant solution for the drum weapon for Battlebots that reduces the number of modes of failure as well as collaborating with teams specializing in electrical, mechanical, and optical properties to create a robotic gripper that encompasses all of these characteristics. Through the competition, Kevin seeks to learn new skills as well as apply the skills he has accumulated through his career.

Sam Yeomens

Sam is a fourth-year Computer Engineering student at Georgia Tech. His choice of major became clear to him one day while playing Minecraft. No joke. Minecraft changed his life. In his free time, Sam loves to pilot hit quad copter. (That was a joke. Free time is a myth.)

Abhirav Joshi
