Project Music Connector

We explore the connections between engineering and music.

We experiment with ways in which technology can allow people without music background to play music, while using music-making as a tool to teach people about technology.


Come see us at MakerFaire Atlanta 2017 (Oct 22) and the Atlanta MakEdu Conference for Educators (Oct 21)!
